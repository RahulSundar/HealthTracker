Diet recommendations for your conditions
💚Anti-Inflammatory Foods
🫐
RA + Thyroid
Turmeric & Ginger
Add to dal, sabzi or warm water daily. Curcumin reduces joint inflammation significantly.
🥬
Thyroid
Cooked Cruciferous Veg
Cook broccoli, cauliflower, cabbage before eating — raw versions interfere with thyroid function.
🫘
Protein + RA
Dal, Rajma, Chole
2–3 servings/day. Legumes provide protein without purine load. Rajma has anti-inflammatory antioxidants.
🌰
Omega-3
Flaxseeds & Walnuts
1 tbsp ground flaxseed in roti or curd. 4–5 walnuts daily. Best plant-based omega-3 for RA.
🚫
Avoid
Reduce these
White sugar, maida, deep-fried snacks, excess chai/coffee (≤2/day). These worsen inflammation and weight gain.

💧
Hydration (8 glasses)
Lubricates joints — critical for RA and knee pain. Also supports thyroid hormone transport.

Protein (50–60g/day)
Prevents muscle loss which is accelerated with RA. Supports metabolism for thyroid function.
🌾
Fibre (25g/day)
Regulates blood sugar (thyroid link), reduces cholesterol, aids weight management.
☀️
Morning sunlight (15 min)
Vitamin D3 is often low in RA patients. Sunlight also regulates sleep and mood.
